# Features and User Stories (Rally-style)

Quick links: [Summary](./summary.md) · [Tech Stack](./tech-stack.md)

ID | Feature | User Story (Title) | Description
---|---|---|---
US001 | Public Landing (SEO) | Access landing page | As a visitor, I can access `/voyageplus` from search without seeing private data.
US002 | Public Landing (SEO) | See public welcome | As a visitor, I see a welcome page that explains private access.
US003 | Public Landing (SEO) | Switch language | As a visitor, I can switch VI/EN on the landing page.
US004 | Entry / Welcome Overlay | Block direct access | As a visitor, a full-screen overlay prevents direct access to content.
US005 | Entry / Welcome Overlay | Display branding | As a visitor, I see logo, headline, subtext, and CTA.
US006 | Entry / Welcome Overlay | Show error state | As a visitor, I see a light error message when access is invalid.
US007 | QR Entry Flow | Open from QR | As a member, I can scan QR on my card to open the landing page.
US008 | Serial Verification | Verify serial | As a member, I can enter my card serial number to verify access.
US009 | Serial Verification | Show status | As a member, I see status Valid/Invalid/Active after submission.
US010 | Serial Verification | Contact admin | As a member, I can contact admin if my serial is invalid.
US011 | Member Portal – Card Overview | View card status | As a member, I can see card name and status (Base/Gold/Diamond).
US012 | Member Portal – Card Overview | View usage summary | As a member, I can see voyages completed and spending progress.
US013 | Member Portal – Card Overview | View last network booking | As a member, I can see last network booking details.
US014 | Member Portal – Charter Pricing | View pricing tiers | As a member, I see yacht pricing by card level.
US015 | Member Portal – Charter Pricing | Compare prices | As a member, I see pre- and post-discount prices.
US016 | Member Portal – Benefits | View current benefits | As a member, I can see benefits for my level.
US017 | Member Portal – Benefits | Preview locked benefits | As a member, I can preview locked future benefits.
US018 | Member Portal – Voyage History | View trips | As a member, I can see trip list with date, yacht, route, type, and status.
US019 | Member Portal – Voyage History | View trip savings | As a member, I see savings for completed trips only.
US020 | Member Portal – Progress & Next Level | See upgrade gap | As a member, I see how much more to reach the next level.
US021 | Member Portal – Progress & Next Level | Prompt next booking | As a member, I can click to book the next voyage.
US022 | Member Portal – Future Privileges | See future labels | As a member, I see greyed/labelled “Available at Gold/Diamond.”
US023 | Admin Access | Admin login | As an admin, I can log in to the admin dashboard.
US024 | Admin Quick Stats | View key stats | As an admin, I can see total cards, active cards, and newest bookings.
US025 | Admin Quick Stats | Open details | As an admin, I can open card list or booking details from stats.
US026 | Admin – Card Management | Create card serials | As an admin, I can create card serials.
US027 | Admin – Card Management | Assign owners | As an admin, I can assign card owners.
US028 | Admin – Card Management | Override status | As an admin, I can update card status manually.
US029 | Admin – Member Management | Manage members | As an admin, I can view and edit the member list.
US030 | Admin – Booking Management | Link bookings | As an admin, I can link bookings to card serial and booking type.
US031 | Admin – Pricing Management | Update pricing | As an admin, I can update charter pricing.
US032 | Admin – News/Notices | Manage notices | As an admin, I can create and edit news/notices.
US033 | Admin – Recent Activity | View activity | As an admin, I can view a 3-day activity timeline.
US034 | Admin – Navigation Modules | Navigate modules | As an admin, I can access Members/Benefits/Reports/Settings.
